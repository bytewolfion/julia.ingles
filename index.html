💬 Conversação Básica

Pratique diálogos simples!

🎯 Diálogo: No Restaurante

Waiter: Good evening! What would you like to eat?

Julia: I would like a pizza, please.

Waiter: What would you like to drink?

Julia: I would like orange juice, please.

Waiter: Thank you! Your order will be ready soon.

Julia: Thank you very much!

🎯 Diálogo: Pedindo Informações

Julia: Excuse me, where is the library?

Person: It's next to the park.

Julia: How can I get there?

Person: Go straight and turn left.

Julia: Thank you for your help!

Person: You're welcome!

🎯 Modal Verbs (Verbos Modais)

Can: I can swim. - Eu sei nadar.

Could: Could you help me? - Você poderia me ajudar?

Should: You should study more. - Você deveria estudar mais.

Must: I must go home. - Eu preciso ir para casa.
